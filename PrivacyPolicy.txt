ROBOOST PRIVACY POLICY
======================
Last Updated: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RoBoost ("we", "our", or "us") collects,
uses, and protects your information when you use the RoBoost Chrome Extension.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. DATA COLLECTION & USAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

We collect the following information when you use RoBoost:

  - Roblox Username & User ID
    Used to identify your account and process boost orders.

  - Session Token
    Used to authenticate your requests to the RoBoost backend.
    This is stored locally in your browser and never shared
    with third parties.

  - Coin Balance & Transaction History
    Used to track your purchases and boost orders within
    the extension.

  - Referral Code Usage
    Used to track referrals and award coins to both parties.

We do NOT collect:
  - Your Roblox password
  - Your payment information (purchases are handled via
    Roblox's own gamepass system)
  - Any personally identifiable information beyond your
    Roblox account details

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the RoBoost
service. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any
third parties for marketing purposes.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES (ROBLOX API)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RoBoost interacts with the following third-party services:

  Roblox API (roblox.com)
  -----------------------
  We use Roblox's public API to:
    - Look up Roblox usernames and user IDs
    - Fetch user avatar thumbnails for display purposes
    - Process gamepass purchases for coin packages

  RoBoost is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially
  connected to Roblox Corporation in any way. Use of the
  Roblox API is subject to Roblox's own Terms of Service and
  Privacy Policy, which can be found at:
  https://www.roblox.com/info/privacy

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3. DATA STORAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Your session token and balance are stored locally in your
browser using Chrome's local storage API. This data remains
on your device and is only transmitted to the RoBoost backend
when making authenticated requests.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
